Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 17, 2026  ·  Last Updated: March 17, 2026

Radiant Windows ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates radiantsolutionsaz.com and communicates with customers by phone, SMS, and email in connection with our window and home improvement consulting services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information and describes your rights.

By visiting our website or submitting your information, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect personal information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Full name
  • Phone number (mobile and/or landline)
  • Email address
  • Home or mailing address
  • Number of windows or doors in your home
  • Any additional details provided in free-text fields or during consultations

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • Referring website or search query
  • Device type and operating system
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies (see Section 6)

1.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from third-party advertising platforms (such as Meta/Facebook or Google) and referral sources, subject to their own privacy policies.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Respond to your inquiry and schedule a consultation or assessment
  • Provide quotes, proposals, and product information
  • Send appointment confirmations, reminders, and follow-up communications
  • Send SMS messages and phone calls related to your inquiry (see Section 3)
  • Send marketing communications about our services and financing options (where you have consented)
  • Process financing applications and coordinate with financing partners
  • Improve our website, services, and customer experience
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and protect the security of our systems

3. SMS and Text Message Communications

⚠️ IMPORTANT — Please read this section carefully before submitting your phone number.

Consent to Receive SMS Messages

By submitting your phone number through any form on our website, you expressly consent to receive SMS text messages from Radiant Windows at the mobile number you provide. These messages may include:

  • Appointment confirmations and reminders
  • Consultation scheduling and follow-up
  • Quotes and proposal notifications
  • Project status updates
  • Promotional offers and financing information

3.1 Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your interaction with us. You may receive up to 10 messages per month. Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

3.2 How to Opt Out

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message we send. You may also opt out by emailing service@radiantsolutionsaz.com or calling (815) 543-3378. After opting out, you will receive one confirmation message and no further SMS communications.

3.3 Help

Reply HELP to any of our text messages for assistance, or contact us directly at service@radiantsolutionsaz.com.

3.4 Carrier Disclaimer

Radiant Windows is not responsible for any delays or failures in SMS delivery caused by your mobile carrier. SMS services are not available in all areas or on all carriers.

3.5 No Mobile Information Sharing

Mobile opt-in data and consent to receive SMS messages will NOT be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. This information is used solely to facilitate direct communications between Radiant Windows and you. We do not sell, rent, or trade your mobile phone number or SMS consent status to any third party.

3.6 A2P 10DLC Compliance

Our SMS communications are sent through an Application-to-Person (A2P) messaging platform in compliance with the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA), the CAN-SPAM Act, and carrier A2P 10DLC registration requirements. Our registered use case is customer care and appointment scheduling for residential window and home improvement services. All messages are sent only to individuals who have provided prior express written consent through our website contact form or verbal opt-in.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

4.1 Service Providers

We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our business, including CRM platforms, email and SMS communication tools, financing partners (subject to your separate consent), website hosting and analytics providers, and installation partners involved in completing your order. These providers are contractually obligated to use your information only as directed by us.

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, your safety, or the safety of others.

4.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your personal information may be transferred to the acquiring entity. We will notify you before your information becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

5. Data Retention

  • Contact form submissions and lead data: up to 3 years
  • Customer records for completed projects: up to 7 years for tax and warranty purposes
  • SMS opt-in records and consent documentation: minimum 5 years (TCPA requirement)
  • Website analytics data: up to 26 months

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies, including essential cookies required for site functionality, analytics cookies to understand visitor behavior (e.g., Google Analytics), and advertising cookies to measure campaign effectiveness (e.g., Meta Pixel). You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described here.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your personal information, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You may have the following rights with respect to your personal information:

  • Access: request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: request that we delete your personal information, subject to legal exceptions
  • Opt-out of marketing: unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time
  • Opt-out of SMS: reply STOP to any text message or contact us directly
  • Data portability: request your personal data in a portable format where feasible

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information in Section 12. We will respond within 30 days.

9. California Residents — CCPA Notice

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA), including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete your information, the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal information, and the right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights.

We do not sell personal information as defined under the CCPA. To submit a verifiable consumer request, contact us at service@radiantsolutionsaz.com.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 18, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy
